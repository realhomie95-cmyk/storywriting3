## 4-beat arcs + Character Lock

### C47001 — F01-08 | Nephew Told He Inherits the Company → The Janitor He Fired Holds Veto Power
- **Setup:** At a family law office, nephew Garrett Caine is told he inherits majority shares of Caine Maritime.
- **Clue:** A trust protector clause requires sign-off from a named individual — whose identity Garrett never checked.
- **Turn:** The trust protector walks in: Eldon Tate, the building janitor Garrett fired six months ago.
- **Payoff:** Eldon was planted as a test by the late patriarch; Garrett's cruelty proves him unfit; Eldon freezes the shares and the board re-elects leadership.
- **Lock:** Garrett Caine 34 (nephew, fitted charcoal suit, cufflinks, smug posture); Eldon Tate 58 (trust protector / former janitor, khaki work pants, blue polo, calm eyes, folder tucked under arm); Attorney Diane Mercer 50 (silver bob, reading glasses on chain). Setting: Wood-paneled law office, Charleston, SC, Wednesday 2pm. Object clue: a manila folder with a wax-seal tab in Eldon's hand.

### C47002 — F06-14 | Wealthy Donors Reserve Front Rows → The Usher They Moved Wrote the Check
- **Setup:** At a newly rebuilt community church, top donors claim front pews and insist the elderly usher move to the back.
- **Clue:** The rebuilding plaque behind the pulpit lists one anonymous donor.
- **Turn:** The pastor reads a thank-you letter naming the anonymous donor — it is the usher, Harold Goss, who gave every dollar of his railroad pension.
- **Payoff:** Harold is invited to sit wherever he chooses; the donors are humbled; the congregation rises for him.
- **Lock:** Harold Goss 74 (usher, gray cardigan over white dress shirt, worn leather shoes, gentle hands holding a hymnal); Vivian Drake 52 (lead donor, silk blouse, pearl drop earrings, possessive grip on pew back); Pastor Lena Odom 45 (warm, holding letter). Setting: Community church sanctuary, Savannah, GA, Sunday 10am, stained-glass light. Object clue: rebuilding plaque on wall behind pulpit.

### C47003 — F12-22 | Stepparent Given Last-Row Tickets → Donor Board Shows Stepparent Named the Scholarship
- **Setup:** At a university graduation, stepchildren give stepmom Iris Navarro a seat in the last row while bio family sits front.
- **Clue:** The commencement program lists a named scholarship presenter.
- **Turn:** The dean calls the scholarship donor to the stage — it is Iris, whose foundation funded the award the stepchild just received.
- **Payoff:** The stepchildren realize the tuition they assumed their father paid was Iris all along; public acknowledgment; mixed (hurt + grace).
- **Lock:** Iris Navarro 44 (stepmom, navy wrap dress, subtle gold necklace, composure despite being sidelined); Tyler Maddox 22 (stepson, cap and gown, stunned); Kevin Maddox 48 (husband/father, uncomfortable in middle). Setting: University auditorium, Madison, WI, Saturday morning, natural light from high windows. Object clue: commencement program in Iris's lap.

### C47004 — F09-24 | Holiday Helper Wraps Gifts in the Den → Her Apron Pocket Holds a Matching Wedding Band
- **Setup:** Wife Camille Breaux hires seasonal help for Christmas gift-wrapping. Husband Jordan insists on the same helper as last year.
- **Clue:** Camille notices the helper, Noelle, wears an apron whose pocket bulges with something metallic.
- **Turn:** While reaching for ribbon, a wedding band rolls from Noelle's apron — identical to Jordan's ring, same engraving date.
- **Payoff:** Jordan married Noelle in a secret courthouse ceremony last spring; Camille's lawyer was already gathering evidence; karma delivered on Christmas Eve.
- **Lock:** Camille Breaux 38 (wife, red holiday sweater, calm exterior, hands folding tissue paper); Noelle Ash 29 (helper, green canvas apron, dark ponytail, nervous eyes); Jordan Breaux 40 (husband, flannel shirt, hovering near den door). Setting: Family home kitchen/den, Raleigh, NC, December evening, warm overhead lighting, wrapped gifts on table. Object clue: gold wedding band visible at apron pocket edge.

### C47005 — F24-03 | Twin Walks the Aisle When Bride Flees → Groom Whispers Wrong Pet Name
- **Setup:** Minutes before the ceremony, bride Maren Collier panics and begs her twin sister Darcy to walk the aisle in her place.
- **Clue:** Groom Jesse Hayes leans in at the altar and whispers a pet name only Maren would know — but Darcy flinches.
- **Turn:** Jesse's whisper reveals to him this is not Maren; Darcy pulls back the veil; the chapel goes silent.
- **Payoff:** The misunderstanding unravels — Maren's panic was not about the groom but about a secret she hadn't yet told him; bittersweet reconciliation when the truth surfaces.
- **Lock:** Darcy Collier 27 (twin/stand-in, identical face to Maren, borrowed veil, trembling hands); Jesse Hayes 29 (groom, black tux, confused half-smile freezing); Maren Collier 27 (real bride, unseen, backstage). Setting: Small wedding chapel, Nashville, TN, Saturday afternoon, natural window light. Object clue: slightly loose engagement ring on Darcy's finger (Maren's ring doesn't quite fit her).

### C47008 — F05-18 | Daughter Opens Family Calendar → Recurring "Client Dinners" Map to One Apartment
- **Setup:** Seventeen-year-old Hailey Park opens the shared family calendar to plan prom logistics and notices a pattern.

- **Payoff:** Jun-seo's calm response — she already suspected; she was waiting for proof; divorce papers served by Friday; karma catches up.
- **Lock:** Hailey Park 17 (daughter, oversized hoodie, laptop open on knees, eyes wide); Jun-seo Park 44 (mother, reading glasses pushed up, still composure masking shock); David Park 46 (father, absent from scene — referenced). Setting: Family den, Boise, ID, Thursday evening, blue laptop glow mixing with warm lamp light. Object clue: laptop screen showing calendar (angled away from camera, not readable).

### C47009 — F27-24 | Wife Finds Storage Unit → Unit Contains Uniform and Civilian Clothes from Same Days
- **Setup:** Military wife Renée Holloway finds a storage unit receipt in her husband Troy's jacket while doing laundry.
- **Clue:** She visits the unit alone; inside she finds neatly hung uniforms next to civilian outfits — each pair photographed together with matching dates.
- **Turn:** The dates correspond to every "deployment" he claimed over three years. He never left the state. The civilian photos show him at a downtown apartment.
- **Payoff:** Renée calls the base — Troy's unit confirms he was administratively discharged eighteen months ago; the marriage and the service were both a lie.
- **Lock:** Renée Holloway 32 (wife, denim jacket over sundress, hand on storage unit latch, stunned expression); Troy Holloway 35 (husband, absent from scene — referenced via photos). Setting: Self-storage hallway, Colorado Springs, CO, Saturday morning, harsh fluorescent corridor light, orange rolling doors. Object clue: Polaroid photos pinned inside the unit door showing uniform + civilian outfit side by side.

### C47010 — F16-02 | Brother Changes Lake House Locks → Locksmith Receipt Shows Sister Holds the Deed
- **Setup:** After their mother's death, brother Craig Ellison changes the locks on the family lake house and texts sister Mara that she is no longer welcome.
- **Clue:** The locksmith asks Craig to verify ownership before rekeying — Craig shows their mother's will, but the locksmith checks county records.
- **Turn:** County records show the deed was transferred to Mara two years ago — their mother quietly gave it to the daughter who cared for her.
- **Payoff:** Mara arrives with the deed and the original keys; Craig must leave; she offers him a guest visit any summer he wants — grace, not revenge.
- **Lock:** Mara Ellison 37 (sister, flannel over tank top, deed folder in hand, quiet confidence); Craig Ellison 42 (brother, polo shirt, arms crossed, flushed); Locksmith Ray 50 (county records printout in hand, stepping back). Setting: Lakehouse front dock/porch, Lake Placid, NY, Saturday morning, mist on water, pine trees. Object clue: new lock still in packaging on porch railing, deed folder in Mara's hand.
