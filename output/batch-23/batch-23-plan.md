### C251 | Embezzlement Slide at VP Celebration

**Seed:** F08-01 — Colleagues chant for the new VP to take a pie → the slide behind her shows embezzled totals in real time

**Engine:** E00 — Humiliation → Reveal

**Arc:**
1. **Setup:** Karissa Vance, 39, is crowned VP of Operations at Meridian Logistics' all-hands in their Dallas, TX headquarters. Three hundred employees applaud as the CEO hands her a crystal award. A projector screen behind the stage displays a congratulatory slideshow.
2. **Clue:** IT analyst Dev Okafor, 27, sits in the third row with his laptop open. He notices the projector feed has been queued with a second deck — one he didn't authorize. A colleague nudges him: "Did you add those slides?"
3. **Turn:** As Karissa raises the crystal, the screen behind her changes. Line items appear: vendor kickback payments totaling $1.4M, routed through shell invoices Karissa approved over eighteen months. The data auto-scrolls. The room falls silent. The source: an anonymous whistleblower who scheduled the slides through the AV system.
4. **Payoff:** Security escorts Karissa off stage. The CEO announces a full forensic audit. Dev is promoted to lead the internal controls team — he'd flagged the anomalies six months ago and was told to drop it.

**Character Lock:**
- KARISSA VANCE — 39, white, auburn hair in a sleek low bun, emerald sheath dress, gold bracelet, stiletto heels; VP of Operations, charming on stage, aggressive behind closed doors
- DEV OKAFOR — 27, Nigerian-American, close-cropped fade, navy blazer over a button-down, glasses, laptop always open; IT analyst, quiet, methodical, flagged the fraud months ago
- ROGER CALLAHAN — 56, white, silver hair, navy suit, CEO; hands Karissa the award, visibly shaken when the slides appear
- Setting: Meridian Logistics HQ, 4th-floor auditorium, downtown Dallas, TX; Friday afternoon all-hands meeting, 300 employees seated, stage with podium and projection screen
- Object clue: second slide deck queued in the AV system; crystal VP award in Karissa's hand
