### C241 | Basement Maid → Owner's Sister Recording Everything

**Seed:** F07-01 — A mistress orders the maid to stay in the basement → the maid removes her wig — she is the owner's sister recording everything

**Engine:** E00 — Humiliation → Reveal

**Arc:**
1. **Setup:** Valentina Rojas, 34, works as a live-in maid at the Kensington estate in Greenwich, CT. The homeowner's girlfriend, Sloane Fairchild, moved in three months ago and has confined Valentina to basement quarters, cut her meals, and forbidden her from the main floor after 6 PM.
2. **Clue:** Sloane hosts a dinner party and orders Valentina to serve wine "from below" without eye contact. Valentina's phone — hidden in her apron — records Sloane instructing the estate accountant to redirect $400K from the family trust to her personal account.
3. **Turn:** The homeowner, Declan Kensington, returns from overseas. He calls a family meeting in the dining room. Valentina walks in — removes her black wig and work apron — revealing herself as Camila Kensington, Declan's younger sister. She has been embedded for eight weeks at Declan's request.
4. **Payoff:** Camila plays the recordings: Sloane's embezzlement, verbal abuse of staff, and forged signatures. Declan terminates the relationship, freezes Sloane's access, and reinstates the housekeeper Sloane fired. The basement Camila endured becomes evidence exhibit A.

**Character Lock:**
- CAMILA KENSINGTON (alias "Valentina Rojas") — 34, Colombian-American, wavy dark hair hidden under a straight black wig, brown eyes, slim; wears a gray maid's uniform and white apron; Declan's younger sister, MBA from Wharton, embedded as undercover operative
- SLOANE FAIRCHILD — 31, white, platinum bob, designer athleisure, Cartier bracelet; Declan's girlfriend of 8 months, controlling, redirecting family funds
- DECLAN KENSINGTON — 38, white, sandy hair, navy blazer; homeowner, frequently abroad for business, asked Camila to investigate

**Engine:** E04 — The Test in Disguise

2. **Clue:** Craig reviews footage hoping to fire Josie. Instead, the cameras capture him and Melanie crushing prescription pills into their morning coffee, passing out on the couch by noon, and leaving the children unsupervised for hours. Josie is always the one feeding, bathing, and comforting the kids.

Josie — Josephine — continues supervising the transition, ensuring the kids know they are safe. The cameras the parents installed become the state's primary evidence.
