## 4-beat arcs + Character Lock summary

### C25001 — F03-25 | Bride Freezes Mid-Aisle -> Groom's Own Voicemail Plays for the Room
- **Setup:** Claire Donnelly walks the vineyard aisle toward Mason Albright; perfect golden-hour wedding.
- **Clue:** Her phone buzzed an hour ago with a voicemail Mason sent to the wrong number — meant for a woman seated in pew three.
- **Turn:** Claire stops mid-aisle, lowers the bouquet, and plays the voicemail on the venue speakers.
- **Payoff (story):** Mason's recorded words expose the affair and a prenup scheme; Claire walks out owning the moment, not the marriage.
- **Lock:** Claire Donnelly 29 (bride, ivory slip gown, phone in bouquet hand); Mason Albright 32 (groom, navy suit, sweating); Brooke Salinas 30 (guest pew three, emerald dress). Object clue: phone screen face-down. Setting: Rosewater Vineyard, Sonoma County, CA, Saturday 6pm.

### C25002 — F08-25 | Forty-Year Man Handed a Gold Watch -> He Returns It and Plays the Board
- **Setup:** Walt Brennan honored at his retirement dinner after 41 years on the floor.
- **Clue:** That morning he overheard the board planning to void the pension fund after he leaves.
- **Turn:** At the podium he sets the gold watch back on the table and plays the recording.
- **Payoff (story):** Pension protected by a clause he co-signed in 1989; the COO who schemed is exposed; Walt retires on his terms.
- **Lock:** Walt Brennan 66 (retiree, gray work shirt under blazer, calloused hands); Derek Pohl 48 (COO, slick suit); Setting: Brennan Tool & Die banquet hall, Akron, OH, Friday evening. Object clue: gold watch on velvet box.

### C25003 — F11-01 | Seated at the Kids' Table -> She Owns the House They're Eating In
- **Setup:** Priya Nadeau placed at the kids' folding table at Thanksgiving by her mother-in-law.
- **Clue:** The deed/mortgage folder sits in her tote; she's quietly paid the note for two years.
- **Turn:** When mocked for "not contributing," she sets the folder on the turkey table.
- **Payoff (story):** She holds title; the in-laws live by her grace; she chooses dignity over revenge.
- **Lock:** Priya Nadeau 34 (daughter-in-law, mustard sweater); Eleanor Vance 61 (mother-in-law, pearls); Tom Vance 36 (husband, torn). Setting: Wheaton, IL dining room, Thanksgiving 3pm. Object clue: manila folder by the gravy boat.

### C25004 — F13-07 | "She Withholds His Medicine" -> The Pharmacy Log Says Otherwise
- **Setup:** In a custody hearing, ex-husband Greg claims Maya never gives their son his meds.
- **Clue:** A pill bottle and a pharmacy pickup log sit in Maya's folder.
- **Turn:** Her attorney enters the pharmacy record — Greg never picked up a single refill.
- **Payoff (story):** Custody shifts; the truth about who actually cares for the boy lands; bittersweet, not triumphant.
- **Lock:** Maya Okafor 35 (mother, gray blazer, composed); Greg Hale 38 (father, gesturing); son Eli 7 (not in courtroom). Setting: Family court, Fort Worth, TX, Tuesday 10am. Object clue: amber pill bottle.

### C25005 — F17-01 | Fined One Inch Over the Line -> The Surveyor Measures the President's Fence

- **Lock:** Reuben Castillo 45 (homeowner, t-shirt, plat map); Karen Doss 52 (HOA pres, clipboard); Surveyor Dana 40 (orange vest, tripod). Setting: cul-de-sac, Henderson, NV, Saturday 9am. Object clue: surveyor tripod/stake.
