### C52001 | Mocked Sister's Art → Hidden Fortune in Paintings

2. **Clue:** Garrett laughs openly, calling Vivian's inheritance "kindergarten finger-paint." He stacks the canvases by the garage for Goodwill. Appraiser Dr. Yuki Tanaka arrives early — Vivian's father scheduled her months ago.

Vivian donates three pieces to the Portland Art Museum in her father's name and keeps the rest. She tells Garrett: "He always said you never looked closely enough."
