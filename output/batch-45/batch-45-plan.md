### C45001 — F02-10 | CEO Dismisses Janitor's Claim → DNA Proves They Are Twins Separated at Birth

**Seed:** F02-10 — A CEO dismisses a janitor's claim → matching markers prove the janitor is his twin, separated at birth

**Engine:** E00 — Humiliation → Reveal | **Tone:** mixed | **Opening:** Dialogue Slap

**Arc:**
1. **Setup:** At Langford Capital in Philadelphia, PA, CEO Marcus Langford, 44, is confronted in the lobby by night janitor Ezekiel "Zeke" Tanner, 44, who says they share a birthday and a birthmark. Marcus laughs it off publicly.
2. **Clue:** Zeke leaves a sealed envelope at the front desk — inside is a commercial DNA test showing a sibling match. Marcus's assistant finds it.
3. **Turn:** Marcus orders a private retest through his physician. Results confirm: full siblings. Hospital records from 1980 show twin boys born to a teenager, one adopted by the Langfords, one placed in foster care — Zeke.
4. **Payoff:** Marcus tracks the adoption records. Their birth mother is alive — living in a care facility in Chester County. He finds Zeke mopping the lobby at midnight and says: "We need to talk. About our mother."

**Character Lock:**
- MARCUS LANGFORD — 44, Black, clean-shaven, tailored charcoal three-piece suit, platinum cufflinks, commanding presence; CEO, raised wealthy, adopted, never told he had a twin
- EZEKIEL "ZEKE" TANNER — 44, Black, same bone structure as Marcus, close-cropped hair with gray at temples, navy janitorial uniform, lanyard badge; night janitor, aged out of foster care, quiet persistence
- Setting: Langford Capital lobby + executive floor, Center City Philadelphia, PA; weekday 8 AM (lobby confrontation) and midnight (resolution)
- Object clue: sealed envelope with DNA results left at front desk; matching crescent-shaped birthmark behind left ear

**Peak-tension (photo):** Zeke standing in the polished lobby holding the envelope toward Marcus who is mid-stride past him with a dismissive hand wave, security guard watching — Beat 2, twist NOT shown.
