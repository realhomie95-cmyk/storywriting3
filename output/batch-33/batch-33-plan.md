### C33001 — F02-19 | PTA Queen Denies Troublemaker → Principal's DNA Test Shows Queen's Husband Fathered the Boy

- **Clue:** Jaylen bears a striking resemblance to Vanessa's husband Brett — same dimples, same left-handedness, same copper-hazel eyes — and the school counselor has noticed.
- **Turn:** A court-ordered paternity test (triggered by a child-support petition Keisha finally filed) confirms Brett Keller is Jaylen's biological father. The test arrives during a PTA meeting.
- **Payoff (story):** Vanessa's public campaign to expel her husband's son collapses; Brett's double life is exposed; Keisha gains child support and Jaylen gains stability. The woman who was humiliated walks out with legal standing.

### C33002 — F04-12 | Town Mourns Hero Lost at Sea → He Docks During the Memorial Service With the Insurance Investigator
- **Setup:** The town of Portland, Maine, holds a memorial for lobsterman Sean Calloway, lost at sea four months ago when his boat was found adrift with no body. His wife Bridget organized the harbor-side service. The Coast Guard declared him presumed dead.
- **Clue:** An insurance investigator named Frank Deluca has been quietly following a trail — Sean's life insurance policy was doubled six weeks before his disappearance, and a cargo vessel logged a rescue at the same coordinates.
- **Turn:** During the memorial service, a fishing trawler enters the harbor. Sean Calloway steps off the bow — thin, bearded, alive — accompanied by Frank Deluca holding a clipboard.
- **Payoff (story):** Sean's "death" wasn't an accident — it was staged to collect insurance. But the reunion is complicated: Sean claims he was coerced by debt collectors, and Bridget must decide whether the man who came back is the same man she mourned.
- **Lock:** Sean Calloway 41 (presumed-dead lobsterman, gaunt face, overgrown dark beard, borrowed yellow rain slicker too large for his thinned frame, duffel bag over one shoulder, squinting at the crowd from the dock); Bridget Calloway 39 (widow/wife, black wool coat, tissue crumpled in one hand, frozen mid-turn toward the dock, expression caught between shock and fury); Frank Deluca 53 (insurance investigator, tan trench coat, clipboard tucked under arm, standing two paces behind Sean with neutral expression); Memorial crowd of ~30 townspeople in winter coats gathered around a flower-draped bollard. Setting: harbor dock, Portland, ME, Saturday 11am, overcast winter sky, gray Atlantic water, lobster boats moored in background, memorial wreath on a dock bollard, breath visible in cold air.
