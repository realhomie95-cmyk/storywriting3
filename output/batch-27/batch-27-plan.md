## 4-beat arcs + Character Lock summary

### C27001 — F01-11 | Board Votes the Founder's Son Out → A Stock Certificate in a Toolbox Gives Him Veto
- **Setup:** Preston Keel III is voted off the board of his late father's furniture company at a contentious shareholder meeting.
- **Clue:** His father's old toolbox sits under Preston's workbench; he's never opened the bottom drawer.
- **Turn:** Preston opens the toolbox's false bottom and finds a notarized stock certificate — veto-class shares.
- **Payoff (story):** He returns to the next meeting, exercises veto control, reinstates fired workers, and the cousin who schemed is locked out.
- **Lock:** Preston Keel III 38 (grandson of founder, flannel shirt, sawdust on jeans, reading glasses pushed up); Victoria Keel-Harmon 52 (cousin, tailored navy sheath dress, pearl brooch, smugness); Attorney Laird 60 (pinstripe, leather folder). Setting: law office conference room, Charleston, SC, Wednesday 2pm. Object clue: battered metal toolbox under the workbench (referenced; stock certificate inside, unseen).

### C27002 — F05-18 | A Daughter Opens the Family Calendar → Every "Client Dinner" Maps to One Address
- **Setup:** Sixteen-year-old Maisie Greer opens the shared family calendar on the kitchen iPad to check soccer practice.
- **Clue:** Recurring events labeled "client dinner" every Thursday all map to the same apartment address on Elm Street.
- **Turn:** Maisie screenshots the calendar and texts her mother, who is at that moment sitting across from a divorce attorney.
- **Payoff (story):** Mother already knew — the calendar evidence becomes Exhibit A; the father's year of lies collapses on his own schedule.
- **Lock:** Diane Greer 44 (mother, cardigan, coffee mug, strained composure); Todd Greer 46 (father, polo shirt, unaware, at gym); Maisie Greer 16 (daughter, soccer jersey, stunned at kitchen island). Setting: suburban kitchen, Raleigh, NC, Thursday 4pm, afternoon light through bay window. Object clue: iPad propped on kitchen counter showing a calendar grid (screen facing away from camera).

### C27003 — F07-15 | Family Complains About the New Aide → She Is a State Inspector Documenting Neglect

Object clue: Zoom recording indicator (small red dot on screen — not readable text, just a colored dot).
