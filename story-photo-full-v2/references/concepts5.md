# Concepts 5 — Viral Drama Genre Bank (30 Families × 25 Seeds)

A fifth concept bank for **story-photo-full-v1/v2**, organized by **viral Facebook drama caption genre** (the story family readers expect before the twist lands).

- **750 seeds** total: 30 families × 25 seeds. Compatible with **concepts4** narrative engines; default pairing **E00** (humiliation → reveal) unless you tag another engine.
- Each seed = one line: **tension setup → payoff promise**. Use as a Phase 0 seed, then build the 4-beat arc + Character Lock.
- **Caption style (v2 default):** viral-long **4000–5000** chars; v1 used 2–3k. Photo shows tension only — full payoff in full story.
- **Mix freely** with settings from `concepts3.md`, recognition from `concepts2.md`, and engines from `concepts4.md`.
- US settings, fictional characters, policy-safe (social betrayal + power reversal, no graphic violence).

## Usage notes

- **Pick a family, pick a seed, then build the 4-beat arc** (Setup → Clue → Turn → Payoff) in Phase 0.
- **Default engine E00** (humiliation → reveal); override with any `concepts4` engine when you want a different emotional machine.
- **Mix freely**: genre from concepts5 + setting from concepts3 + recognition from concepts2 + engine from concepts4.
- **Rotate** families, antagonists, and opening styles across a batch — no consecutive duplicates.
- **Twist never appears in the photo** — only in caption + full story (unless you explicitly pair an `action-in-photo` engine from concepts4).
- Keep characters fictional and US-based; honor the Google content policy in `nano-banana-2.md`.
