# Concepts 3 — Expanded Setting Bank (50 New Groups)

A third concept bank for the **story-photo-full-v1** pipeline. Every group here is a **new setting/context** not covered by `concepts1.md` (categories A–M) or `concepts2.md` (groups 1–12).

- **Engine:** ordinary/dismissed person is publicly humiliated or blocked → a clue, witness, or arrival flips the power. **Twist stays out of the photo**; it pays off in caption + full story.
- **~600 seeds across 50 groups** (10–15 ideas each).
- Each idea = one line: a humiliation/block → the reveal. Use as a Phase 0 seed, then build the 4-beat arc + Character Lock.
- US settings, fictional characters, Google-policy safe (social humiliation + power reversal, no graphic violence).

**Source tags:** `concepts3 / Group 07` or `concepts3 #G07-4`.

## Group 01 — Airports & Air Travel

Gates, jet bridges, first-class cabins, baggage counters, control towers.

1. **The Standby Sneer**: A gate agent loudly denies boarding to a shabby standby passenger, then the captain steps out and salutes him as the retired chief pilot who trained her.
2. **The First-Class Glare**: A businesswoman demands a man in a worn jacket be moved from the seat beside her, until the purser reveals he is the engineer who designed the aircraft.
3. **The Taped Suitcase**: A check-in clerk mocks an old woman's taped-up luggage, then a customs officer recognizes the seal showing she is a returning ambassador.
4. **The Wheelchair Wait**: Staff leave a veteran in a wheelchair stranded at the gate, until the airline's CEO arrives to personally award him for saving a downed crew decades ago.
5. **The "Wrong Lounge"**: A lounge host throws out a quiet man in sneakers, then the manager faints seeing his card — he owns the alliance the lounge belongs to.
6. **The Crying Baby Complaint**: A first-class flyer demands a young mother be moved to the back, until the flight attendant recognizes her as the nurse who saved his daughter.
7. **The Translator Dismissed**: A clerk laughs at an immigrant struggling with forms, then a federal agent asks the same man to translate for a stranded delegation.
8. **The Old Boarding Pass**: A teen is mocked for carrying a yellowed boarding pass, until a pilot sees it is from the rescue flight that carried his family out of a disaster.
9. **The Tarmac Mechanic**: Executives ignore a grease-stained mechanic flagging a fault, then the grounded jet's inspection proves he prevented a catastrophe.
10. **The Overhead Bin Fight**: A passenger shoves an elderly man's bag out of the bin, until the man opens it to reveal medals and the captain stops the cabin to honor him.
11. **The Upgrade Refusal**: A desk agent refuses a tired soldier an upgrade for "looking unkempt," then her supervisor reveals corporate's standing order to seat him in first.
12. **The Lost Child Hero**: Security drags away a humming janitor near the gate, until a missing toddler runs to him — the only adult who calmed her, and her grandfather is the airport director.

## Usage notes

- **Pick a group, pick a seed, then build the 4-beat arc** (Setup → Clue → Turn → Payoff) in Phase 0.
- **Mix freely**: combine a setting from concepts3 with a recognition mechanic from concepts2 (heirloom, scar, lullaby, photo) for fresh hybrids.
- **Rotate** settings, antagonist types, and opening styles across a batch — no consecutive duplicates.
- **Twist never appears in the photo** — only in caption + full story.
- Keep characters fictional and US-based; honor the Google content policy in `nano-banana-2.md`.
