# Concepts 2 — Expanded Concept Bank

Total: 240 English short-drama scenario seeds in 12 concept groups.

Use these as raw story concepts for story-photo-full-v1 (4:5 photo prompts, captions, full story articles, or batch HTML packages). Each scenario is built around public humiliation, emotional misjudgment, a visible clue, and a final identity or power reversal.
